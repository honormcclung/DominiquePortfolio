About the Podcast

Welcome to The Bear Minimum, a Christian late-night podcast where faith meets authenticity. Hosted by Bear Goodspeed, this show explores life’s biggest questions, shares inspirational stories, and dives into the heart of what it means to live a faith-filled life in today’s world.

What to Expect

  • Engaging discussions on faith, culture, and everyday life.
  • Late-night vibes with a Christian perspective.
  • Special guests sharing their personal journeys.
  • Moments of prayer, reflection, and inspiration.

Join the Conversation

Tune in every week for new episodes filled with humor, hope, and honest discussions. Whether you're winding down your day or seeking a faith-filled perspective, The Bear Minimum is here to keep you company.
